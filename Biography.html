Hei!

To keep it brief, I am a Norwegian/Lithuanian that grew up on military bases around Europe. Up to this point, I have lived in Spain, Italy, Norway, and now the United States. Throughout my upbrining, I've always loved to play all kinds of sports. Soccer (or football as we say in Europe) was my main sport when I was younger, where I played the position of goalie. Besides soccer, I also loved X-Country skiing, running, and tennis. Once I reached adequate maturity, I realized that I had to switch avenues into a more slow-paced and boring sport: golf. My current handicap is in the low 3's (on good days, at least). As a final fun fact, I hiked to the top of the tallest volcano in Europe during an eruption!
